Pixar’s Light and Magic Talk

November 1st | 5:15PM-7:00PM | HUM 111

Christophe Hery has been in the graphics industry since 1987, and has been working as a Senior Engineer at Pixar since 2010. In addition to sharing his experience in the industry, Christophe will address the technology behind Merida’s hair from Brave to the underwater lighting in Finding Dory.
