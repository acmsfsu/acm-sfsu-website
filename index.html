About Us


The Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) at SFSU is one of the largest student run tech communities in San Francisco and its parent university.

SFSU ACMs objective is to develop the skills of SFSU’s tech community in programming, computer science research, design, and leadership. We hope to expand SFSU’s tech community by providing the resources and hard work it needs to flourish.

Our Vision is to cultivate the next generation of technology leaders by promoting the spirit of innovation and establishing a culture of creativity
