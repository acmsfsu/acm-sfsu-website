Tech Internship Workshop

Wednesday, February 14th | 6:00PM | HUM118

Want to land an internship at a tech company this summer? This workshop has got you covered! In this session, @timbeiko, a CS undergrad who has worked at Shopify and will be joining Google this summer, will cover how to find internships, increase your chances of getting an interview, prep so that you're ready for it and how to show your best self on the big day! He'll first walk through his experience interviewing with top tech companies, and then answer any questions you might have.
